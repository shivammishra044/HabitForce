### Description

### Default State
**Enabled** (ON by default)

### What It Does
When enabled, the system will automatically use your forgiveness tokens at 11:50 PM each day to protect habits you missed. This helps maintain your streaks without manual intervention.

## How It Works

### When Enabled (Default)
- âœ… System checks your habits at 11:50 PM daily
- âœ… Identifies habits with active streaks that weren't completed
- âœ… Automatically uses forgiveness tokens (if available)
- âœ… Protects longest streaks first

### When Disabled

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I change this setting anytime?
**A**: Yes! Toggle it on/off whenever you want. Changes take effect immediately.

### Q: What if I run out of tokens?
**A**: Auto-forgiveness won't work if you have 0 tokens. You'll need to complete habits to earn more tokens (future feature).

### Q: Which habits get protected first?
**A**: Habits with the longest streaks are protected first. For example, a 45-day streak is protected before a 10-day streak.

### Q: Will I be notified when tokens are used?
**A**: Yes! You'll receive a notification showing which habits were protected and how many tokens were used.

### Q: Can I manually use tokens even with auto-forgiveness enabled?
**A**: Yes! You can manually use tokens anytime through the habit details or Goals page.

### Q: Does auto-forgiveness respect quiet hours?
**A**: The job runs at 11:50 PM regardless of quiet hours, but notifications respect your quiet hours settings.

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: Tokens Not Being Used Automatically
**Check**:
1. Is auto-forgiveness enabled in Settings â†’ Notifications?
2. Do you have available tokens (> 0)?
3. Do you have habits with active streaks?
4. Did you miss any habits today?

**Solution**: Verify all conditions above. If everything looks correct, contact support.

### Issue: Wrong Habits Being Protected
**Explanation**: This is by design. The system protects habits with the longest streaks first to maximize value.

**Solution**: If you want to control which habits are protected, disable auto-forgiveness and use tokens manually.

### Issue: Didn't Receive Notification
**Check**:
1. Are in-app notifications enabled?
2. Are you within quiet hours?
3. Check your notification preferences

**Solution**: Adjust notification settings or check the Notifications page for missed alerts.
