## Tracking Progress

### Completing Habits

#### Daily Completion
1. View your habit list on the dashboard
2. Click the completion button (checkmark)
3. Watch the celebration animation!
4. See your XP and streak update

#### Late Completion
- Complete habits from previous days
- May affect streak (unless using forgiveness token)
- Still earns XP
- Marked as "late completion"

#### Undo Completion
- Click the completed checkmark again
- Available within 24 hours
- Removes XP earned
- Adjusts streak accordingly

### Understanding Streaks

**Current Streak**
- Consecutive days you've completed the habit
- Resets to 0 if you miss a day (without forgiveness)
- Displayed prominently on habit cards

**Longest Streak**
- Your personal best
- Never decreases
- Motivational milestone

**Streak Milestones**
- üî• 7 days: Week Warrior
- üî• 30 days: Month Master
- üî• 100 days: Century Club
- üî• 365 days: Year Champion

### Forgiveness System

#### What are Forgiveness Tokens?

Forgiveness tokens let you maintain your streak even when you miss a day. They're earned through consistency and can be used strategically.

#### Earning Tokens
- Earn 1 token every 7 consecutive days
- Automatic reward at midnight
- No limit on total tokens
- Visible in your profile

#### Using Tokens

**Manual Application:**
1. Go to the habit with a broken streak
2. Click "Use Forgiveness Token"
3. Select the missed date
4. Confirm application
5. Streak is maintained!

**Automatic Application:**
- System automatically applies tokens when beneficial
- Preserves long streaks intelligently
- Notification when auto-applied
- Can be disabled in settings

#### Token Strategy
- Save tokens for long streaks
- Use early to prevent breaks
- Don't hoard unnecessarily
- Balance earning and using

## Gamification Features

### Experience Points (XP)

#### Earning XP
- **Base XP**: 10 points per habit completion
- **Streak Bonus**: Extra points for longer streaks
- **Level Multiplier**: Higher levels earn more XP
- **Challenge Rewards**: Bonus XP for challenges
- **Milestones**: Special XP for achievements

#### XP Calculation Example
```
Habit completion at Level 5 with 15-day streak:
Base XP: 10
Streak Bonus: (15 / 7) * 5 = 10.7
Level Multiplier: 1 + (5 * 0.1) = 1.5
Total XP: (10 + 10.7) * 1.5 = 31 XP
```

## Tips & Best Practices

### Building Habits Successfully

**Start Small**
- Begin with 1-3 habits
- Make them easy to complete
- Build consistency first
- Add more gradually

**Be Specific**
- "Exercise 30 minutes" not "Exercise more"
- "Read 10 pages" not "Read more"
- "Drink 8 glasses water" not "Drink water"

**Stack Habits**
- Link new habits to existing ones
- "After coffee, meditate 5 minutes"
- "After dinner, walk 10 minutes"

**Track Consistently**
- Complete habits at same time daily
- Set reminders
- Use forgiveness tokens wisely
- Review progress weekly
